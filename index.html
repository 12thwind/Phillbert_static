About Phillbert Solutions

Founded with a mission to bridge the gap between
academic institutions and the professional world.

Founded with a mission to bridge the gap between academic institutions and the professional world, Phillbert Solutions brings specialized expertise to university career services departments looking to enhance their operations and impact.

We understand the unique challenges that career centers face - limited resources, evolving employer needs, shifting student expectations, and rapidly changing technologies. Our comprehensive approach addresses these challenges by combining industry best practices with innovative solutions tailored to your institution's specific needs.

With years of experience working with career services teams across the country, we bring valuable insights and practical strategies that deliver measurable results - whether that's increased student engagement, stronger employer partnerships, or more efficient operational processes.

Our Services

We help university career services teams optimize their operations and strengthen connections between students and employers through comprehensive solutions.

  • Job Board Management
  • Corporate Relations
  • Program Development
  • Recruitment Event Platforms
  • Analytics & Strategy
  • Technology Solutions

Job Board Management: We help university career services teams optimize their job boards for maximum effectiveness. Our approach focuses on streamlining posting processes, improving search functionality, and enhancing student engagement to increase utilization rates.

Corporate Relations: Develop strategic partnerships with employers and recruiters that benefit your students and institution. We provide frameworks for establishing meaningful connections, maintaining engagement, and creating value-driven relationships that last.

Program Development: Design and implement career-focused programs that strengthen connections between students and employers. From mentorship initiatives to industry-specific networking events, we help you create impactful experiences.

Recruitment Event Platforms: License and support cutting-edge platforms for career fairs and recruitment events, both virtual and in-person. Our solutions streamline scheduling, enhance engagement, and provide valuable analytics to measure success.
